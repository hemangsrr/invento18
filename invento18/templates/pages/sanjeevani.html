ABOUT SANJEEVANI

WHY SANJEEVANI?

We believe it's the responsibility of a student not just to pursue his education but also to make the society he is in better. As the name suggests we aim to render a new life to the needy sections of the society and act as an oasis of hope for the needy sections of the society.

OUR INITIATIVES

Go Green!

Reduce Reuce Recycle has always been the mantra to sustainability. We sanjeevani believe in making ourselves eco-friendly before asking others to do. A campaign to minimize the use of flex and other platics within the campus is the best way to begin Promote use of eco-friendly products such as paper pen and bags both within and outside the campus is a need of the hour A tree planting drive within and outside the campus we aim to plant and take care of 50 trees in total

Wheels of hope

Not everyone is gifted with the ability to move around freely. We believe gifting wheelchairs could act as Wheels of hope for a better tomorrow. If not all, lives of a few could be changed

Colour the corners

Every nook and corner has been blessed with a bus stop in every locality. But the impoverished condition of those is dilemma faced by every citizen. Sanjeevani with the help of young artists have come up with an idea to revamp the grimy bus stops to a maze of art
